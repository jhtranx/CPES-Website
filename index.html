About Us

We are the Computer Engineering Society, a social and educational community for the Computer Engineering majors of California State Polytechnic University, San Luis Obispo.

We began our life apart from the Computer Science branch of IEEE just a few years back, and are still slowly severing our ties.

We look forward to providing a fostering community conducive to both the personal and professional development of our members, while keeping close ties to industry.

We hope to see you at our meetings!

Membership

The Computer Engineering Society is an IRA club, meaning we are sponsored by the department for students of the department. We have no membership fees, but we do have cool shirts you can purchase for $12 during our general meetings!

By joining the Computer Engineering Society you are connecting with other students and members of industry passionate about our major. We aim to offer many opportunities for professional networking, challenging computer related projects, and community building social events. We strive to give our major a positive image in the eyes of the school and the local community through community service.

Here is a list of the benefits of joining CPEs, which we believe is well worth the modest cost:

  • Reduced price on club activities and products
  • Discount on end-of-year banquet
  • Workshops
  • A variety of social events
